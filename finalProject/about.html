Message From the President

2020 marks the year that most would rather forget. While the complexities of quarantines and difficulties of life caused substantial hardship throughout our community, the strength of our small-town spirit was emboldened. With the help of programs from the federal, state, and local government, the Duvall Chamber of Commerce provided our local, small businesses with the tools and capital needed for many to survive. My name is Josh Lowell, and the owner of the law offices of Magnuson Lowell, P.S., and it will be my pleasure to continue leading the Duvall Chamber of Commerce into a bright new tomorrow.

When times are tough, resiliency is built. From our gourmet restaurants and boutique shops to our skilled service providers and medical professional, the Duvall Chamber of Commerce will build upon the successful programs previously introduced. The local business community has expressed interest in new exciting and inventive platforms to showcase the wide array of offerings unique to small-town life. With the help of our dedicated Directors, staff, and loyal Members, we will expand and reach these new horizons in 2021.

The Chamber, its incredible Board, and experienced staff will help provide the information and resources necessary for Members to prosper. With the vast community support at our back, the Snoqualmie Valley will grow, providing our 200 Members with the supplies, energy, and support to boost its customers and bottom line in the new year. We pledge as the Duvall Chamber of Commerce to Grow, Connect, and Prosper.

The Duvall Chamber of Commerce welcomes businesses of all shapes and sized non-profit organizations and entrepreneurs looking to learn, network, and expand. Join us in 2021 for another amazing year!

Joshua B. Lowell
President, Duvall Chamber of Commerce
Law Offices of Magnuson Lowell, P.S.
