Become a Chamber Member

Benefits of Membership

  • Your business or organization is listed in the upcoming printing and online postings of Duvall Chambers Business Directory & Visitor Guide (delivered to over 11,000 area homes and businesses) and distributed in Chamber Welcome Bags to every new Duvall homeowner.
  • Prospective customers can easily find you through your listing on the Duvall Chamber of Commerce online business directory.
  • Welcome post (with your business Facebook tag, if applicable) on the Duvall Chamber Facebook page.
  • You can promote your business or organization through participation in the Chamber's sponsorship opportunities.
  • Your up-to-date business info is kept on file by the Chamber for responses to inquiries from prospective customers.
  • Your business or organization is showcased on our Duvall Chamber Facebook Page throughout the year and promoted when you post your Announcements or events on our Chamber Events/Announcements Calendar as a Chamber Membership perk. Events area ALSO sent out through a weekly email blast, to the 1500+ opted-in subscribers, for even more visibility to you!
