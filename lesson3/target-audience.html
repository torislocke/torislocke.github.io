Persona: Jane Smith

Jane Smith is a single, twenty-eight (28) year old remote worker for a Silicon Valley-based tech company. She works long hours as a college recruiter searching for graduates of computer science with entry-level programming experience. Her company supplied her with the latest technology, including an iPhone 11 pro, which is her primary device for interacting with recruits, her peers and the web.

Jane wants a website that provides her with local weather and event information to plan time with her friends and community.
