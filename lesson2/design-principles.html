Tico Shaving Website

The main idea about the Rule of Thirds is to divide the layout into nine equal sections with the two horizontal and two vertical lines. Each of these sections will take about 33,33% of the horizontal space and the same percent of the vertical space. That’s why they are called “Thirds”. Intersections of the lines form focal points where the main elements of the composition should be arranged. What can be simpler: https://www.motocms.com/blog/en/rule-of-thirds-use-website-design/
