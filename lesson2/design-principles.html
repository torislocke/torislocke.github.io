Contrast

Disney

disney.com

Disney uses contrasting values to create a visually appealing and easy to read website. High contrast is created through the use of dark background images overlaid by bright white text.

Rule of Thirds

Skyline Films

skylinefilms.tv

Skyline Films creates visual interest through the use of the Rule of Thirds. Imagine the viewport divided into nine equal sections by two equally spaced horizontal and vertical lines. You will note Skyline Films placed the key compositional elements along these imaginary lines or their intersections.
